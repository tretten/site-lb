    All the logos in the Logos & Brands (L&B) archive are grouped into visual categories such as circles, crosses, stars, crowns, animals, people, and other shapes and are shown in black and white to emphasize the visual form further. The logos are from various companies, countries, and historical periods. L&B is genuinely international and features the world's outstanding identity designers. Examples are drawn not just from the USA and Europe but also from Australia, South Africa, the Far East, Israel, Iran, South America, and Eastern Europe. Contributing design firms include Pentagram, Vignelli Associates, Chermayeff & Geismar, Wolff Olins, Landor, Total Identity, Ken Miki & Associates, and dozens of highly creative, emerging studios.

    Each logo comes with similar logos and symbols that make your creative process more straightforward. You can use the collection for inspiration, analytical purposes, and brand research.
